Welcome To Besant Technology

Besant Technologies is a well-known IT training institute in India that offers both classroom and online courses in various technologies such as Full Stack Development, Data Science, Cloud Computing, DevOps, Python, Java, and many more. It has multiple branches mainly in Chennai and Bangalore, and it promotes itself as a center for training with real-time projects and placement assistance. The institute provides flexible learning options including weekday, weekend, and online classes, aiming to help students and professionals upgrade their skills.
